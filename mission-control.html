⚡ Quick Start — say these in Cowork

/today
Generate your morning brief — calendar, inbox priorities, portfolio pulse. Click to copy → paste into Cowork.
/research [TICKER]
Deep-dive on any stock — news, analyst consensus, thesis fit check. Click to copy "Research " then add the ticker.
/wrapup
Update the Drive spine (NOW / DECISIONS / OPEN) and log today's session. Do this before closing Cowork.
/today update [note]
Append a timestamped note to today's brief without regenerating. Click to copy then add your note.

Drive Spine — context bridge between Cowork and Chat

NOW
Current state — what's active today, project status, recent decisions. Overwritten each session.
DECISIONS
Append-only log of locked decisions and their reasoning. Never edited — only appended.
OPEN
Live list of unresolved questions and next steps. Updated every session.

Connect these Google Docs to a Claude Chat Project → Opus reads your full context without re-explaining anything.

Skills

📋 Morning Brief Say "generate my morning brief"
Pulls live data from your calendar and inbox, reads your portfolio file, and produces a single structured brief. Saves to projects/daily-digest/brief.md.
1Pulls today's events from Google Calendar
2Scans Gmail inbox for unread threads requiring action (ignores automated notifications)
3Reads your portfolio file and flags any positions needing attention
4Writes the brief and displays it in chat
## Morning Brief — Monday, 22 June 2026
### Portfolio pulse AAPL: +12% · TSLA: -8% Watch · ...
### Today's calendar 10:00 — Team standup · 14:00 — Client call
### Inbox priorities 3 emails needing action
### Action items Carry-forwards + new items
📈 Stock Research Say "research AAPL"
Searches the web for current price, recent news, and analyst consensus, then checks whether the stock fits your investing criteria from portfolio.md. Output saved to projects/investments/dashboard.md.
1Reads your investing philosophy from portfolio.md
2Web searches: current price, 52-week range, last 7 days of news, analyst consensus
3Assesses thesis fit: Strong / Neutral / Weak against your stated criteria
4Writes bull case, bear case, and verdict: Watch / Hold / Add / Trim / Pass
5Appends to dashboard.md and displays in chat
## Research: AAPL — 22 June 2026
Thesis fit: Strong · Price: $211.40 · 52-week: $164.08–$237.23
Recent: WWDC announcements · Services growth · AI integration roadmap
Verdict: Hold — thesis intact, no catalyst to add at current valuation

How to use it

Create a file called YYYY-MM-DD-description.md in autonomous-builder/pending/:

# 2026-06-22-weekly-portfolio-summary.md

## Goal
Write a one-page weekly summary of my portfolio performance.

## Output
- Format: md
- Filename: 2026-06-22-portfolio-week.md

## Data sources
- Read projects/investments/portfolio.md
- Web search current prices for each ticker

## Constraints
- One page, no filler

Say "check the build queue" to run it manually, or enable scheduled 30-minute checks in Cowork Settings → Scheduled Tasks.

projects/daily-digest/brief.md Today's morning brief — overwritten each run Regenerate with "generate my morning brief"
